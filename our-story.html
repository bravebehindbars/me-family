Treatments available to pregnant women!

There are many types of treatment.

It all depends on what fits you and your unborn child safely and best. There is volunteers that will gladly be there if you need someone to just talk to, about anything that may be troubleing you. They can atleast give you some suggestion on places to call or go to, or just be there to listen.

There are many out patient therapies, or in patient facilities.

The out patient places will give you the schedule that works for you. You will be able to choose what makes you more comfortable, in group therapy or one/one therapy. They are also very open for you to switch therapist for your comfort level. The in patient facilities are more structured. You will stay at the facilities day and night for the amount of days the facilitie requires.

  • out patient tends to be more convient for people for families.
  • In patient tends to work better for the people that are having a hard time being in the community and letting go of their addictions.

There is medication assisted treatment.

The medication assisted treatments under the supervision of a doctor can be very safe. It is never a good idea to try to use medication assisted treatment on your own. You could really harm your child or/and yourself. The harm can be as little as some development problems or as risky as causing death.

It is never a good idea to detox on your own. Best safe way to detox is to admit yourself in a in-patient facility. They would be able to moniter you and your baby to make sure there is no adverse affect and cause health problems to you or your baby.

How to find treatment centers.

You can ask your regular doctor, some are more comfortable with their personal doctor.

  • You could ask a friend if they know. Most people do know, even if they don't use or are still using.
  • There is ads for treatment facilities that will give you a number to call. Usually if you call one they can direct you in the correct direction. Same with calling any medicl facilities.
  • Your OB.is always a good way to find the right treatment.
